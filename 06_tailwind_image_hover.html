WELCOME TO DEOMALI

Deomali is a mountain peak in the Chandragiri-Pottangi subrange of the Eastern Ghats in Odisha, India. At 1,672 meters, it's the highest peak in Odisha and one of the top hill stations in the state. Deomali is located near Koraput town in the Koraput district, about 60 kilometers away by road!

WELCOME TO KORAPUT

Koraput, the beautiful district also known as the 'Switzerland of Odisha', is crammed with large waterfalls, expansive skies, majestic hills, and spectacular wildlife. It is in the agro-climatic zone of India, consisting of red, red, and yellow mixed soil that is rich in iron and aluminium
